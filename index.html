Rummy Assistant (Two-Decks + Single-Card Missing)

Enter exactly 13 cards total. For each suit (♣, ♦, ♥, ♠), type your ranks as a continuous string (no commas/spaces), using:
`A 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 T J Q K` (where **T** = “10”). Then choose your Wild-card rank (or leave blank), and specify how many pure Jokers (JOK) you have (0–2).

Note on Two Decks: You may see the same rank-suit twice (e.g. two “8♣”). For any set or sequence, duplicates of the same suit do not count twice in that set. All grouping logic (pure/impure sets, pure/impure sequences) and leftover-point logic remain as before.

Updated—Missing Cards for Pure Sequences:
Now, if you have two cards in the same suit and no pure sequence in that suit:

  • Gap = 1 (e.g. 8♣ & 9♣): missing cards are 7♣ and 10♣.
  • Gap = 2 (e.g. 4♣ & 6♣): missing card is 5♣.
  • Gap ≥ 3 (e.g. 6♣ & 9♣): ignore that pair (no missing cards).
If there is already a pure sequence (length ≥ 3) in a suit, no missing cards are reported for that suit.
