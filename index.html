Rummy Assistant (List All Pure & Impure Sequences)

Enter the ranks you have for each suit (comma‐separated), choose your wild card rank, and specify how many pure Jokers (JOK) you have (0–2). A “wild card rank” (e.g. 8) means that any 8 of any suit can act either as a normal “8” in a pure run of its suit, or as a wildcard in an impure sequence. The program will:

  1. List all pure sequences (length ≥ 3) found in each suit, and remove those cards.
  2. Then repeatedly list the optimal impure sequence (length ≥ 3) that minimizes leftover‐card points (using available Jokers + wild‐rank cards), removing each from the pool.
  3. Any remaining cards are “leftover” and will count toward your point total. Wild‐rank cards that never joined any group count as 0 points; pure Jokers (JOK) always 0.
  4. Total leftover points are capped at 80.
