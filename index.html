Rummy Assistant (Sets + Sequences + Optimal Impure)

Enter your cards by suit, choose the wild card rank, and specify how many pure Jokers (JOK) you have (0–2). The assistant will:

  1. List all pure sequences (length ≥ 3) and remove them.
  2. List all pure sets (three or more of the same rank) and remove them.
  3. Then repeatedly find the optimal impure sequence (length ≥ 3) that minimizes leftover‐points, removing each.
  4. Any remaining cards whose rank ≠ wild‐rank will be shown as “leftover” (and counted). Any wild‐rank or JOK leftover count as 0 points and are not shown.
  5. Points for leftover: A/10/J/Q/K = 10, 2–9 = face value, capped at 80.
